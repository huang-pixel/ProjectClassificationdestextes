kebab

   noun
   Save Word
   ke·​bab | \ kə-ˈbäb How to pronounce kebab (audio) also ˈkā-ˌbäb How to
   pronounce kebab (audio) \
   variants: or kebob or less commonly kabob

Definition of kebab

   : cubes of meat (such as lamb or beef) marinated and cooked with
   vegetables usually on a skewer
